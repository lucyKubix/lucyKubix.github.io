## <a href="https://www.finecheese.co.uk/" target="_blank">The Fine Cheese Co</a>
### <a href="https://github.com/kubixmedia/the-fine-cheese-co" target="_blank">Repo</a>

* ### Metafield-driven product info:
Nutrition, ingredients, allergens, and store/serve content managed via metafields and surfaced through accordions and a full-screen modal.
* ### Dietary badges system:
Metaobject-powered dietary icons with tooltips, shown on PDPs and product cards with flexible display modes.
* ### Bundle contents display:
Bundle items rendered from metafield lists, including per-item quantities and linked nutritional modals.
* ### Events calendar:
Custom events section with date, location, pricing, animations, and flexible layouts.
* ### Delivery countdown logic:
Web component calculating delivery dates with weekday rules, blackout periods, and live countdown timers.
* ### Type & origin blocks:
Product blocks pulling cheese type and origin from metafields with configurable styling.
* ### Draft product filtering:
Metafield-based draft logic to hide products from collections/search while allowing direct access.
* ### Quick Add drawer:
AJAX product drawer with variant support, accessibility handling, and request cancellation.
* ### Variant-aware galleries:
Swiper galleries that re-filter and reinitialise based on the selected variant.
