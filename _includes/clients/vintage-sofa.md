## <a href="https://www.vintagesofa.co.uk" target="_blank">Vintage Sofa</a>
### <a href="https://github.com/kubixmedia/Vintage-Sofa" target="_blank">Repo</a>

* ### High-complexity architecture:
Large custom JS footprint (custom-async.js ~5.8k lines) powering advanced AJAX features, variant logic, drawers, and metafield-driven behaviour, requiring strong Liquid and JS expertise.
* ### Klaviyo integration:
Onsite tracking, back-in-stock notifications, custom-styled modals via theme settings, and detailed cart/add-to-cart event tracking.
* ### Reviews.io integration:
Review widgets across PDPs, collections, and homepage with custom carousels, filtering, verified badges, media support, and brand-aligned styling.
* ### Advanced cart drawer:
AJAX cart with undo actions, delay timers, free-shipping progress, real-time updates, quantity rule validation, and variant metafield display (dimensions, delivery).
* ### Live search system:
Predictive search drawer with debouncing, multi-resource results, suggestions, overlay mode, and mobile-optimised UX.
* ### Complex variant selection:
Swatches, buttons, and dropdowns with availability logic, disabled states, dynamic pricing, variant images, and metafield syncing.
* ### Recently viewed products:
localStorage-based tracking with configurable limits, deduplication, FIFO handling, and editor-safe initialisation.
* ### Quickshop drawer:
Full product preview drawer with complete variant logic, selling plans, AJAX cart support, and module re-initialisation.
* ### Metafield-driven features:
Variant-specific USPs, dimensions, delivery metaobjects, file downloads, and JSON-based data for fast switching.
* ### Filtering & sorting:
Advanced collection filters with drawers or static layouts, price sliders, active filters, show-more logic, and clean URL parameter management.
